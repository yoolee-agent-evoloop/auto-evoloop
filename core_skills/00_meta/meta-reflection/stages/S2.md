# S2 Reflection: Attribution Quality

Use after case analysis produces `case_report.md` and `feedback.json`.

## Checks

- Expected and observed behavior are both stated.
- Evidence is quoted or referenced, not invented.
- The primary failure mode is named only when evidence supports it.
- Alternative hypotheses are listed when uncertainty remains.
- Repair hints do not jump directly to broad rewrites.

## Stop Conditions

- The report cannot identify where the failure occurred.
- The finding depends on missing trace fields.
- Multiple cases are conflated into one unsupported cause.
- The proposed repair hint is stronger than the evidence.
