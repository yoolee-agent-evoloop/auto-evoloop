# S4 Reflection: Validation and Release Readiness

Use after `auto-fix-executor` produces `optimization_report.md`.

## Checks

- Baseline and candidate results are comparable.
- Regression checks are reported, including "none found" when applicable.
- The report names commands, inputs, and outputs.
- Residual risks are visible.
- The final decision is one of: accept, continue, rollback, return to planning.

## Stop Conditions

- The report claims improvement without score evidence.
- Only happy-path cases were evaluated.
- Regressions are omitted or hand-waved.
- The change cannot be rolled back or explained.
