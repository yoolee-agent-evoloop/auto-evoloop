# S4 Reflection: Validation and Release Readiness

## Purpose

Check whether execution evidence supports the final decision.

## Inputs

- `optimization_report.md`.
- Baseline and candidate score outputs.
- `progressive_log.json`.

## Outputs

- S4 reflection note.
- Recommendation to accept, continue, roll back, or return to planning.

## Procedure

1. Confirm baseline and candidate runs are comparable.
2. Check score deltas and regression review.
3. Confirm commands and inputs are named.
4. Confirm residual risks are visible.
5. Confirm final decision is explicit.

## Human Gate

Stop when success is claimed without score evidence, regressions are omitted, or
rollback is unclear.

## Failure Modes

- Non-equivalent runs are compared.
- Only passing cases are evaluated.
- Regression findings are hidden.
