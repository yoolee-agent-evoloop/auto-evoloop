# S1 Reflection: Evidence Readiness

## Purpose

Check whether `analysis_manifest.json` is ready for S2.

## Inputs

- Draft or confirmed manifest.
- Selected eval rows.
- Trace and runner output references.

## Outputs

- S1 reflection note.
- Recommendation to proceed, revise S1, or stop for review.

## Procedure

1. Check stable case IDs.
2. Confirm expected behavior fields exist.
3. Confirm trace files map to selected cases.
4. Confirm privacy status is explicit.
5. Confirm `review_status` reflects human scope review.

## Human Gate

Stop when selected cases, schema, trace coverage, or privacy status is unclear.

## Failure Modes

- Selected cases are ambiguous.
- Trace files cannot be matched.
- Private or real data appears in public-bound artifacts.
