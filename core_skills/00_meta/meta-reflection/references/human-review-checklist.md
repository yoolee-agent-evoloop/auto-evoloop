# Human Review Checklist

## Scope

- Is the experiment goal clear?
- Are selected cases representative of the goal?
- Are out-of-scope cases explicitly excluded?

## Evidence

- Are inputs, traces, scores, and reports named?
- Can another reviewer find the same files?
- Are assumptions and missing data visible?

## Privacy

- Are examples synthetic or public-approved?
- Are credentials, internal endpoints, private repository names, and real user
  data absent?
- Has trace cleaning been treated as a helper rather than a guarantee?

## Decision

- Is the next action clear?
- Does the decision match the evidence strength?
- Is the next stage fully unblocked?
