# Code of Conduct

We want Auto-evoloop to be a constructive and professional open-source space.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and specific in discussion.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Avoid sharing private, identifying, or sensitive data.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about risks and tradeoffs.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, disclosure of private information, or intentionally introducing sensitive data into the repository.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect the project and its community.
